What Orange County drivers aren't being told
The System Was Built
For Them. Not You.

Insurance companies have spent decades conditioning Orange County drivers to call them first and follow their shop recommendation without question.

01
The Preferred Shop List
Your insurer recommends shops enrolled in their Direct Repair Program (DRP). These shops agree to prioritize cheapest and fastest repairs — measured by severity and cycle time — not your vehicle's safety.
02
OEM Certified Shops Are Hidden
Shops certified by your vehicle's manufacturer — OEM certified — follow manufacturer repair procedures. They exist across Orange County. They're kept at the bottom of the preferred list on purpose.
03
The Out-of-Pocket Trap
When a non-certified shop and your insurance disagree on repair procedures, you pay the difference — on top of your deductible. In Orange County where repair costs run high, that gap hits hard.
04
The Gold Standard Exists
A small number of shops inside every DRP network are also OEM certified — protecting you from getting caught in the middle. These are the Orange County shops we surface. They exist. They're just buried.
